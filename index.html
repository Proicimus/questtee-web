For Parents

Safety baked into every pixel.

We believe in teaching teenagers financial independence, responsibility, and community building, but safety is our absolute priority.

🛡️

Location Privacy

Exact locations are never shown to Quest Givers. Only an approximate radius is displayed until a Quest is officially accepted.

⏰

Smart Limits

School and life come first. Quests are capped at 3 hours maximum, limited to 2 per day, and blocked between 9PM and 7AM.

💳

Cashless & Secure

No awkward cash exchanges. Payments are processed securely via Stripe. Under 18s require a parent/guardian sign-off to receive payouts.
